How to order:

1.) Choose an item from our product line up, then click Add to cart to add item on your shopping list.
( You can check your chosen items by clicking the button on the navigation tab. )

2.) Click the button and fill out the customer information sheet (i.e. Name, Address, etc..).
You'll also have a preview of your orders in this window too.

3.) Once you've finalized your orders you can click on the Copy Orders button to copy the customer information sheet and orders, then click on Checkout to be re-directed facebook messenger/chat page.

4.) On Facebook messenger/chat, press 'Ctrl + V' to paste your orders.
You can also manually copy/confirm your orders by typing in the item name(s)/pcs - If you do so, please don't forget to include your Name, Address, Contact number, preferred mode of payment and date of payment.
